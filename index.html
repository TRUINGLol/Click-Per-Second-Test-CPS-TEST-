What is CPS Test ?

The CPS TEST aka click test is more likely a timepass game where you can set the highest clicks per second score. Below there are simple steps you need to follow:

  1. To start the click speed test game, Start clicking on the area what we called a clicking pad, where "Click to Start" has written.
  2. Quickly after clicking the button, start clicking with your mouse as fast as you can in the given timeframe. A timer above the clicking pad will show the time passed.
  3. Once the time runs out, the final score of the player will be presented. The score will be presented as what CPS score the player has achieved!

It is just the frequency of clicks to a time unit. It means you increase your frequency of clicks tend to increase your score. While the game is set to default time for 5 seconds, players can switch to other time varieties from the menu on the right sidebar of the page. It includes mainly - clicks per 10 seconds, clicks per 15 seconds, clicks per 30 seconds, clicks per 60 seconds (1 minute),

How do we calculate CPS?

To find out how many clicks you get per second, we divide the total number of clicks by the total duration in seconds.

Here's the formula you can use:

Clicks Per Second (CPS) = Total Number of Clicks / Total Time in Seconds Let's go through an example to illustrate how to calculate CPS: Example: If you click 30 times in 5 seconds, what is your CPS? Example: Total Number of Clicks = 30, Total Time in Seconds = 5 CPS = 30 / 5 = 6 CPS
